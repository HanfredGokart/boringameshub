What is this place?

BoringGamesHub is a personal collection of browser games — built one by one, completely offline, no servers needed. Every game lives in its own folder and gets added to this page when it's done.

⚡

Instant Play

Every game opens directly in your browser. Click a card, the game loads. That's it.

📁

Simple Structure

Each game lives in its own folder inside games/. The library grows as new folders are added.

🌑

Offline First

No internet required after opening the page. Everything runs locally on your machine.
